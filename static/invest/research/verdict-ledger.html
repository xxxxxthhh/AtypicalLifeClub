Verdict Ledger

Every published stance scored against its per-layer benchmark over the same window, so opinions carry a track record.

Relative return vs each report's benchmark is the score; a call that trails its benchmark is a miss even when it is up. Calibration is grouped by benchmark bucket, and the book-level SMH reference line keeps the all-calls comparison explicit. Migration rows (the legacy→v5 re-labelling) stay scored vs SMH. No red/green — the sign carries direction.
