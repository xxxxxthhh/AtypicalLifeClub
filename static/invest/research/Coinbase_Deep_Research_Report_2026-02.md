## 1. Business Overview

### What Is Coinbase

Founded in 2012 and publicly listed on Nasdaq via direct listing in 2021, Coinbase is the largest regulated cryptocurrency exchange in the United States. In May 2025, it was added to the S&P 500 ‚Äî a historic milestone for a crypto-native company.

### How It Makes Money

Coinbase's revenue mix is undergoing a structural transformation:

**Transaction Revenue (~55-60%):**
- Retail trading fees (core profit driver, significantly higher take rates than institutional)
- Institutional trading (81% of volume, but lower fees)
- Derivatives trading (significantly expanded post-Deribit acquisition)

**Subscription & Services Revenue (~40%):**
- USDC interest income (largest single item, $355M in Q3 2025)
- Staking rewards
- Custody fees (custodian for 9 of 11 spot Bitcoin ETFs)
- Coinbase One subscription
- Base chain-related revenue

**Q3 2025 Revenue Breakdown:**
- Total Revenue: $1.87B (QoQ +25%, YoY +55%)
- Transaction Revenue: $1.05B (QoQ +37%)
- Subscription & Services: $747M (QoQ +14%, YoY +34%)

### Key Business Lines

**1. Base Chain (Layer 2)**
- Ethereum-based L2 network; Q4 2025 stablecoin transaction volume reached $3 trillion (+121% QoQ)
- Surpassed Ethereum and Tron to become the largest network by stablecoin volume
- $3.7B USDC held on Base

**2. Deribit Acquisition ($2.9B, completed August 2025)**
- World's largest crypto options exchange
- 2024 trading volume exceeded $1 trillion, $30B open interest
- Contributed $52M revenue in Q3 2025
- Makes Coinbase a comprehensive platform spanning spot, futures, perpetuals, and options

**3. USDC Ecosystem**
- $73.7B in circulation (Q3 2025)
- Coinbase receives 100% interest income on on-platform USDC + 50% off-platform (revenue share with Circle)
- Coinbase holds ~23% of total USDC supply

## 6. üêª Bear Case (Steel-Manned)

**Core Thesis:** Coinbase is fundamentally still a crypto cycle stock. The current "diversification" narrative masks its root dependence on crypto market sentiment. In a crypto winter, all growth stories freeze.

**Supporting Evidence:**

**1. The Brutal Reality of Crypto Cycles**
- 2022 lesson: revenue from $7.8B ‚Üí $3.1B (-60%), net loss of $2.6B
- BTC down from $126K peak to ~$70K (-44%); if it continues falling, trading volumes will further shrink
- Retail trading revenue (highest-margin business) can drop 80%+ in bear markets
- Forward P/E ~30x indicates market already expects significant earnings decline

**2. SBC Is an Invisible Value Destroyer**
- $2.1B/year SBC is 29% of revenue ‚Äî this is not "free"
- Share count grew from ~190M at listing to ~263M (+38% dilution)
- On SBC-adjusted real earnings, valuation is far less cheap than it appears
- Management itself is selling ‚Äî Armstrong consistently reducing via 10b5-1 plans

**3. Competitive Pressure Cannot Be Ignored**
- Binance global share 40%+ vs Coinbase 5-6%
- Kraken planning 2026 IPO, will directly compete for U.S. compliant market
- Traditional brokers (Robinhood, Schwab) entering crypto trading
- DEXs continuously eroding share
- Long-term downward trend in trading fee rates is irreversible

**4. USDC Interest Income Fragility**
- If the Fed cuts rates significantly, USDC interest income will shrink materially
- This is Coinbase's largest "stable" revenue source, but entirely dependent on rate environment
- Tether (USDT) remains the absolute dominant stablecoin

**5. Regulatory Risk Not Fully Eliminated**
- California DFAL Act effective July 2026, compliance costs may surge
- Nevada enforcement action against prediction markets (Feb 2026)
- State-level regulatory fragmentation risk
- Customer class action lawsuits still ongoing

**Downside Scenario:** If BTC falls to $40-50K and crypto enters a deep bear market, Coinbase revenue could retreat to $4-5B, net income shrinks dramatically, stock price could fall to $80-100.
