## 1. Business And Chain Role

Vistra is an integrated generation and retail power platform with nuclear, gas, coal / transition assets, and retail power capabilities. AI data centers do not need only "green power"; they also need dispatchable capacity that can come online quickly and match load volatility. VST therefore has more merchant power and gas-capacity beta than CEG.

| Asset / variable | Current readout | AI-infra meaning |
|------------------|-----------------|------------------|
| Nuclear fleet | Meta 20-year agreements cover Ohio / Pennsylvania nuclear assets | Low-carbon baseload and hyperscaler PPA validation |
| Gas capacity | Cogentrix adds about 5,500MW | Dispatchable capacity for data-center load growth |
| PJM / Texas exposure | Capacity value rises in data-center-heavy regions | Direct beneficiary of grid shortage and capacity markets |
| Leverage | Q1 net debt about $19.3B | Expansion upside and financial risk coexist |
| Valuation | EV about $70B | Cheaper than CEG, but riskier |

## 2. Bull / Bear Frame

Bull case:

1. **AI data centers need dispatchable capacity.** Nuclear is suited to long-term baseload; gas is suited to speed and dispatchability. VST owns both.
2. **Meta agreements validate direct hyperscaler power procurement.** Long-term PPAs and uprates can reprice existing nuclear assets.
3. **Cogentrix is scarce capacity.** About 5,500MW of gas generation becomes more valuable if data-center demand keeps rising.
4. **Valuation is below CEG.** If power scarcity persists, VST has higher beta.

Bear case:

1. **Leverage is higher.** Q1 net debt was about $19.3B, and acquisitions / capex consume cash.
2. **Gas exposure adds commodity and emissions risk.** Gas prices, carbon policy, and customer decarbonization constraints can affect profits.
3. **PPA details are opaque.** Meta agreement pricing, cost allocation, and regulatory path still need verification.
4. **Market value is sensitive to AI power demand.** If AI capex or data-center growth slows, merchant power premiums can roll over quickly.

## 5. Conclusion

Vistra is the high-beta comparison to CEG. CEG is the steadier nuclear PPA anchor; VST is the nuclear plus gas plus merchant capacity anchor.

The current classification is **high-risk watch / power dashboard**. VST is useful for validating whether AI power demand spreads into dispatchable capacity and capacity markets, but it is more exposed to leverage, gas, regulation, and cyclicality. Covering it prevents the power layer from becoming only a clean nuclear story when real data centers may need a mixed power stack.
